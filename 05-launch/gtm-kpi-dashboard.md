## Weekly Review Agenda (30 minutes)
1. **Metrics Review (10 min)** -- vs. targets and vs. prior week
2. **Wins (5 min)** -- What's working?
3. **Blockers (5 min)** -- What's not working?
4. **Actions (10 min)** -- What are we changing this week?

## Monthly Deep Dive Agenda (60 minutes)
1. **Performance vs. Plan (15 min)**
2. **Funnel Analysis (15 min)** -- Where are we leaking?
3. **Customer Insights (15 min)** -- Win/loss, NPS feedback
4. **Strategic Adjustments (15 min)** -- What's changing next month?
