## Discovery Questions by Stage

### Stage 1: Problem Identification
- "Walk me through your current support workflow from ticket creation to resolution."
- "What percentage of tickets require human intervention today?"
- "How has your support volume trended over the past 12 months?"
- "What's the average cost per ticket in your organization?"

### Stage 2: Impact Quantification
- "How do support delays affect your customer retention and expansion?"
- "What happens to CSAT during high-volume periods (product launches, outages)?"
- "How much time do your agents spend on repetitive vs. complex issues?"
- "What would you do with the capacity if Tier-1 issues were automated?"

### Stage 3: Solution Vision
- "If you could automate 70% of repetitive tickets, what would that mean for your team?"
- "How would your CS team structure change with AI handling first response?"
- "What metrics would you need to see to justify this investment?"

### Stage 4: Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We already have [competitor]" | "Most of our customers came from [competitor]. What gap are you trying to fill?" |
| "AI isn't accurate enough" | "Let's run a pilot with your anonymized data. Our average accuracy is 85%+ after training." |
| "Too expensive" | "Let's calculate your cost per ticket and ROI timeline. Most customers see payback in 3 months." |
| "Security concerns" | "We're SOC 2 Type II certified. Happy to complete your security questionnaire and have our CTO speak with yours." |
| "Need to wait until next budget cycle" | "Let's start a pilot now with a small team. We can align the full contract with your fiscal year." |

## Demo Framework

### Pre-Demo (15 minutes before)
- [ ] Account research completed
- [ ] Custom demo environment prepared
- [ ] Attendee roles identified
- [ ] Primary objective defined

### Demo Structure (30-45 minutes)
1. **Recap & Agenda (3 min)** -- Confirm their priorities
2. **Problem Validation (5 min)** -- Show understanding of their pain
3. **Solution Overview (5 min)** -- High-level capabilities
4. **Live Demo (20 min)** -- Focus on their top 3 use cases
5. **ROI Discussion (5 min)** -- Quantify the value
6. **Next Steps (3 min)** -- Define concrete next actions

### Post-Demo
- [ ] Follow-up email within 2 hours with recap
- [ ] Share relevant case study
- [ ] Schedule technical deep-dive if needed
- [ ] Update CRM with notes and next steps

## Deal Stage Definitions

| Stage | Definition | Exit Criteria | Average Duration |
|-------|------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Prospect | Qualified ICP, no active conversation | Discovery call scheduled | 1-2 weeks |
| Discovery | Understanding pain and priorities | Business case identified | 1-2 weeks |
| Demo | Solution presentation completed | Technical requirements confirmed | 1-2 weeks |
| POV/Pilot | Proof of value in their environment | Success criteria defined and met | 2-4 weeks |
| Negotiation | Commercial terms discussion | Pricing and terms agreed | 1-2 weeks |
| Closed Won | Contract signed | Kickoff scheduled | 3-5 days |
| Closed Lost | Decision made not to proceed | Loss reason documented | -- |
