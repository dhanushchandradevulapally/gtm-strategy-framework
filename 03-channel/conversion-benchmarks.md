## Key Conversion Optimization Levers

1. **Onboarding flow** -- Every additional step reduces conversion by ~10%
2. **Time to first value** -- Sub-1-hour activation drives 3x higher conversion
3. **Social proof** -- Case studies increase trial-to-paid by 15-25%
4. **Pricing page clarity** -- Transparent pricing increases qualified signups
5. **In-product prompts** -- Contextual upgrade prompts outperform emails 5:1
