# Case Study Template

## Title Format
[Company] [Achieves/Reduces/Increases] [Metric] with [Your Product]

Example: "FinTech Co. Reduces Support Costs by 60% with ResolveAI"

---

## Structure

### 1. Customer Overview (1 paragraph)
- Company name, industry, size
- Business context and market position
- Why they needed a solution

### 2. The Challenge (2-3 paragraphs)
- Specific pain points before your product
- Quantified impact of the problem
- Why existing solutions failed
- Internal pressure or triggering events

### 3. The Solution (2-3 paragraphs)
- How they discovered your product
- Implementation process and timeline
- Key features/capabilities used
- Integration with existing stack

### 4. The Results (bullet points with metrics)
- **Metric 1:** X% improvement in [KPI]
- **Metric 2:** $X saved/generated
- **Metric 3:** X hours/time saved per week
- **Metric 4:** X% increase in [outcome metric]

### 5. Customer Quote
> "[Quote from customer about their experience, specific outcomes, and what they value most]"

-- [Name], [Title], [Company]

### 6. About [Your Company]
1-2 sentences about your company and mission.
