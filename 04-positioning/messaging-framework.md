## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### We Are:
- **Expert** but approachable
- **Confident** but not arrogant
- **Innovative** but reliable
- **Bold** but honest

### We Use:
- Active voice
- Concrete examples over abstractions
- Customer language over industry jargon
- "You" and "Your" over "We" and "Our"

### We Avoid:
- Superlatives without proof ("best", "most advanced")
- Buzzword soup ("synergy", "leverage", "holistic")
- Negative messaging about competitors
- Overpromising on capabilities
