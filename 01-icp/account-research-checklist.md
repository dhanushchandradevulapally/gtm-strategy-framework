# Account Research Checklist

Complete this checklist before every Tier 1/Tier 2 sales call.

## Company Context
- [ ] Company size (employees, revenue, growth rate)
- [ ] Industry and sub-vertical
- [ ] Recent news (funding, acquisitions, leadership changes)
- [ ] Competitive landscape (who are their competitors)
- [ ] Business model (B2B, B2C, marketplace, etc.)

## Technographics
- [ ] Current helpdesk/support tools
- [ ] CRM platform
- [ ] Communication channels (chat, email, phone, social)
- [ ] API/integration maturity
- [ ] Cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, GCP)

## People
- [ ] Decision maker identified (title, tenure, background)
- [ ] Influencers mapped (IT, operations, finance)
- [ ] Mutual connections on LinkedIn
- [ ] Previous employers (might know our product)
- [ ] Content they've shared/engaged with recently

## Pain Signals
- [ ] Support job postings (quantity, seniority, duration open)
- [ ] Glassdoor reviews mentioning support team challenges
- [ ] Social media complaints about customer service
- [ ] Recent pricing changes (might indicate cost pressure)
- [ ] Support volume indicators (public case studies, forums)

## Competitive Intel
- [ ] Current vendor and contract end date (if known)
- [ ] Previous vendor evaluations
- [ ] Tools they've trialed (G2/TrustRadius reviews)
- [ ] Competitors they mention in their marketing

## Engagement History
- [ ] All previous touchpoints with our company
- [ ] Content consumed (whitepapers, webinars, blog posts)
- [ ] Trial activity (if applicable)
- [ ] Email engagement rates

## Call Preparation
- [ ] Personalized outreach hook identified
- [ ] Relevant case study selected
- [ ] Technical requirements anticipated
- [ ] Objection handling prepared
- [ ] Next step defined before call starts
