## ICP Validation Checklist

Before finalizing your ICP, validate with:
- [ ] 10+ customer discovery interviews
- [ ] Win/loss analysis of last 20 deals
- [ ] Review of support ticket themes (if applicable)
- [ ] Sales team feedback on deal quality by segment
- [ ] CAC and sales cycle data by segment

---

## Review Cadence
- **Quarterly:** Reassess firmographic criteria based on win/loss data
- **Annually:** Full ICP refresh with market research
